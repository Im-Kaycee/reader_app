// What it does

BUILT
DIFFERENT.

📡
RSS Aggregation

Pulls from 12+ hand-picked RSS feeds across 6 categories. No algorithm. No ads. Just the feeds you chose.

📶
Offline Reading

Articles cached locally for 7 days. Read on the bus, on a plane, underground. No connection needed.

🕳️
Rabbit Hole

Every day, one article you'd normally scroll past gets surfaced. A long read. Something different.

🔖
Bookmarks

Save articles permanently. Bookmarks survive cache clears and live on the device forever.

🔇
Mute Sources

Toggle any source off from settings. Your feed, your rules. Mute preferences persist across restarts.

🔍
Full Search

Search across all cached articles by title, source, or category. Instant results, no server needed.

// How to install

GET IT ON
YOUR PHONE.

Reader is a free Android APK. No Play Store needed. Four steps and you're reading.

01
↓
Download the APK

Tap the download button below. The APK file will save to your phone's Downloads folder.

02
⚙
Allow Unknown Sources

Go to Settings → Security → Install unknown apps and allow your browser or file manager.

03
📂
Open the File

Open your Downloads folder, tap reader-v1.apk and hit Install when prompted.

04
📖
Start Reading

Open Reader from your home screen. Pull down to load your feeds. You're in.
